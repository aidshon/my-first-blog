Read online on bliss

Bliss is a popular literary samizdat. In our electronic library you can read online books of popular genres. We have a mobile version of the site, so you can easily read books online from your phone or tablet. On samizdat you can read romance novels, fantasy, science fiction, female prose, etc. Our handy reader is designed for both windows, and for the android system, and for a regular computer. Our free e-book library is continuously updated with new works from popular authors and young talents. Are you interested in fanfics? Humor? Different genres of works? Then you came to the right place, because the best e-books are waiting for you here!
